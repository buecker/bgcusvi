Our Mission

To enable young people, especially those who need us most, to reach their full potential as productive, caring and responsible citizens.

Who We Are

Our programs work. In a recent survey taken by our club members, they report:

  • 88% are at grade level and 90% engage in physical activity 3 or more days per week.
  • 3 out of every 4 expect to attend at least four years of college.
  • 52% participate in Club-based service once a week or more.

The Boys & Girls Clubs of St. Thomas/St. John, Inc. is changing the future for our community’s youth.
